Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your gallery hours?

Our gallery is open on Weekends from 4:00PM to 8:00PM.

2. Do you charge admission fees?

No, admission to our gallery is free for all visitors.

3. Can I purchase artwork online?

Yes, many of the artworks displayed in our gallery are available for purchase online through our website.

4. Are all artworks displayed for sale?

While most artworks in our gallery are available for purchase, some pieces may be part of temporary exhibitions and not for sale.

5. Is photography allowed in the gallery?

Yes, photography for personal use is permitted in the gallery. However, flash photography and tripods are not allowed.
